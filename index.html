About Prince

Prince exploded on the scene and there have been few artists capable of creating a body of work as rich and varied. During the '80s, he emerged as one of the greatest talents of the rock & roll era, capable of seamlessly tying together multiple different styles of music such as pop, funk, folk, and rock. He released a series of groundbreaking albums while constantly touring, produced albums, wrote songs for many other artists, and recorded hundreds of songs that remain unreleased in his vaults.

Prince frequently experimented with his music in ways that other contemporary artists would not do and was able to blend many diverse styles into a cohesive whole. Each album released demonstrated a remarkable stylistic growth and musical diversity as a result of his experimenting with different sounds. Click here to learn more about Prince
